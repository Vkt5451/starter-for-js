About us

Located in the heart of Sacramento, our bakery has been proudly serving the community for over 20 years. We specialize in crafting the most authentic, hand-rolled bagels using time-honored baking traditions and the finest ingredients. Whether you're an early riser or just craving a fresh, warm bagel, we're here for you — we open our doors bright and early every morning to bring you that perfect start to your day.
